About SBIT

SWARNA BHARATHI INSTITUTE OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY

Swarna Bharathi Institute of Science & Technology (SBIT) sponsored by Sri Swarna Bharathi Educational Society, founded on the noble principles of (S)incerity, (B)enevolence, (I)nnovation & (T)echno-savy, coupled with managerial expertise, have completed 17 years of glorious service in promoting quality and excellence in technical education.

Equipped with dedicated and Benevolent Management with lofty ideals, the Institutions have been striving to synthesize Science & Technology for the well-being of student community and prepare highly qualified and well-trained personnel to meet the demands and challenges of the market forces.

To establish and excel in the arena of education, aimed at preparing young Technosavvy engineers with practical knowledge in technology, by fortifying them with Innovative idea with the help of Sincere faculty members dedicated to their professional services, SBIT found its pinnacle slowly but steadily in a very short span of time and transformed into a brand name.

It has not only grown as the most reputed and sought-after institutions on the horizons of Technical Education in the District of Khammam but also created an indelible mark among the institutions of higher learning in the State of Telangana.

Courses We Offer

In our college we provide different courses they are Btech,Diploma and Mba.Here is the description of the courses below

Btech

In our B.Tech program, we offer specializations in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE),Civil Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE), Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE), and Mechanical Engineering (MECH). These branches provide students with a comprehensive education in their respective fields, equipping them with the necessary knowledge and skills to excel in their chosen domain.

Diploma

In our Polytechnic program, we offer courses in Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Electrical Engineering, and Electronics Engineering.The Mechanical Engineering course focuses on the design, manufacturing, and maintenance of machinery and mechanical systems. The Civil Engineering course covers areas such as construction, infrastructure development, and project management. The Electrical Engineering course focuses on electrical systems, power generation, and distribution.

MBA

MBA programs offer diverse courses in finance, marketing, strategy, leadership, and more. Students gain a foundation in accounting, economics, and statistics. Courses in behavior, marketing, and operations enhance understanding. Corporate finance, strategic management, and entrepreneurship foster innovation and decision-making. Areas like international business, supply chain management, and ethics prepare for a globalized, responsible environment. MBA courses develop well-rounded business professionals.
